Who are we?.

Teku Charitable Foundation, a nonprofit organisation dedicated to empowering the boy the boychild and improving the lives the underprivileged. Our Mission is to provide support, resources and opportinity to help vulnerable boys and young men reach their full potential.

Love

At the heart of our mission is love—a love that transcends boundaries, breaks barriers, and unites us in a shared vision of a better world. Love inspires us to see the potential in every individual, to offer a helping hand, and to build communities rooted in kindness and compassion..

Compassion

Compassion is the heartbeat of our mission—a deep, unwavering care for the struggles and dreams of others. It drives us to reach out, to listen, and to take action where it matters most. At our core, we believe that compassion is not just an emotion; it is a call to action that has the power to change lives and create a ripple of hope.

Charity

Charity is more than an act of giving—it’s a testament to the power of humanity to come together for a greater purpose. It is the bridge that connects those who can give with those in need, creating a cycle of kindness that uplifts everyone it touches.

Water Project In Kenya

Bringing clean, safe, and sustainable water to communities across Kenya—transforming lives, improving health, and unlocking potential where it’s needed most.

Shelter Giving

Providing safe, dignified, and stable shelter for vulnerable families—restoring hope, protecting lives, and building stronger, more resilient communities.

Warri Medical Outreach

In the heart of Warri, Delta State, our recent medical outreach brought much-needed care and compassion to hundreds of individuals and families. With the support of dedicated healthcare professionals and generous volunteers, we provided free medical consultations, medications, and health education to underserved communities.
